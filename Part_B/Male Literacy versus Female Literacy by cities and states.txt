title: Male Literacy versus Female Literacy by cities and states

Explain: 	This scatter chart analyze Male Literacy versus Female Literacy by cities and states
	Each point in the chart represent city, and the Male + Female literacy percentage.
	Point color indicate the cities state.
	
	In general, we can see that in most of the cities Male Literacy percentage is higher than female percentage.
	Moreover, there are cities like Aizawal where percentage are very high (99%), and cities which percentage are quiet low (65%),
	and we can see that state Kerala has very good Literacy percentage.
